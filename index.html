About Our Church

Our Story

Calvary Harvest Church was founded in 1991 with a simple yet powerful vision: to create a community where prayer is not just practiced, but lived. For over three decades, we have been a beacon of hope, faith, and spiritual growth in our community.

We believe in the transformative power of prayer and the life-changing message of Jesus Christ. Our doors are open to all who seek truth, healing, and a deeper relationship with God.

Through worship, fellowship, and service, we strive to be the hands and feet of Christ in our community and beyond.

Our Mission

To equip believers to grow in their faith, live purposefully, and impact the world through prayer, worship, and compassionate service.

Our Vision

To be a thriving spiritual family where every person experiences God's love, discovers their purpose, and uses their gifts to glorify Him.

Our Values

Faith, Prayer, Community, Service, Integrity, and Love. These core values guide everything we do as we seek to honor God in all aspects of life.
